About

There are many great places to use and share data and information from your domain of knowledge. Technology is just a tool to do so. kmapper is a one-person company dedicated to helping you curate, organize and publish your data and information. kmapper's favorite place to do so is the web and web technologies are the best tools for this.

kmapper is rooted in the open project kmapper.org. A tool making use of open access research articles to visualize subjects in an interdisciplinary context.

Principles

kmapper advocates short development cycles and is based on the principles of Feedback, Simplicity, and Change.

Feedback

Having contact with you in more frequent iterations, you get a clear insight into what is being developed. You can give feedback and steer the development as needed.

Concise documentation of each iteration and written feedback avoids costly meetings.

Simplicity

Starting from the simplest solution, a product takes into account the current requirements.

With many little steps made, you have more control over the development process and the product being developed.

Change

As something is better understood over time, your requirements might change.

Changes are embraced and new requirements are planned for the next iteration.
