About kmapper

kmapper is a singe-person company bringing your real-world project to digital life. The Web is the best place to use and share data and information from your domain of knowledge. Web technologies are just the tools to do so.

kmapper is rooted in the open project kmapper.org. A tool making use of open access research articles to visualize subjects in an interdisciplinary context.

Principles

kmapper advocates short development cycles and is based on the principles of Feedback, Simplicity, and Change.

Feedback

Having contact with you in more frequent iterations, you get a clear insight into what is being developed. You can give feedback and steer the development as needed.

Concise documentation of each iteration and written feedback avoids costly meetings.

Simplicity

Starting from the simplest solution, a system takes into account the current requirements.

With many little steps made, you have more control over the development process and the system being developed.

Change

As a problem is better understood, your requirements might change.

Changes are embraced and new requirements are planned for the next iteration.
